About Me

Software Engineering Salerno Lab

Active member of the SeSa Lab, which I consider a second family. Here I develop innovative research in software engineering.

I am a Ph.D. graduate from the University of Salerno, specialized in Software Engineering. My research focuses on understanding how software reuse impacts code quality and reliability in systems that evolve over time.

I consider this a fascinating topic with many useful implications for both academia and industry. My goal is to contribute to creating more robust and maintainable software systems.
